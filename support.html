Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Download the Hash it Out app from the App Store or Google Play Store. Open the app and tap "Sign Up". Enter your username, email, and password, then verify your email address using the verification code we send you. Once verified, you can log in and start debating!

Yes! Hash it Out offers a free tier that allows you to participate in 1 active debate with unlimited comments and access to all core features including AI summaries and evidence analysis. You can upgrade to Mid (£9.99/month for 5 debates) or Unlimited (£14.99/month for unlimited debates) plans if you want to engage in more simultaneous debates.

After logging in, tap the "+" button to create a new debate. Choose a topic, write your opening statement, add supporting evidence (optional), and decide whether to challenge a specific user or open it to anyone. You can also browse existing open debates and apply to join them!

Debates

In a turn-based debate, participants take turns presenting arguments. Each participant has 7 days to respond to their opponent's argument. This structure encourages thoughtful, well-researched responses instead of hasty reactions. You'll receive a notification when it's your turn, and debates automatically timeout if a participant doesn't respond within the time limit.

If you don't respond within 7 days, the debate will be marked as timed out and your opponent may be declared the winner by default. It's important to only join debates you have time to participate in. You can follow debates without participating if you just want to observe!

No, arguments cannot be edited once posted. This ensures the integrity of the debate and prevents participants from changing their positions after the fact. Make sure to review your argument carefully before submitting!

Either participant can propose to end the debate at any time. Both parties must agree to end it, or the debate will continue. Debates can end when consensus is reached, arguments are exhausted, or by mutual agreement. You'll receive an AI-generated summary when the debate concludes.

Features

Our AI (powered by GPT-4) analyzes debates in two ways: (1) Evidence Bias Analysis - evaluates the quality and potential bias of sources on a 1-5 scale, and (2) Debate Summaries - generates concise summaries of the entire debate showing key arguments from both sides. This helps you and observers evaluate arguments more objectively.

Unlike simple "like" buttons, Hash it Out uses a nuanced 1-5 sliding scale voting system. This allows the community to express how persuasive they find each argument. Votes are displayed as aggregate statistics, showing the average rating and distribution across the scale.

Yes! You can follow both users and debates. Following a user lets you see their new debates and activity. Following a debate sends you notifications about new turns, comments, and updates even if you're not a participant. This is great for observing debates on topics you care about.

You'll receive push notifications for important events: when it's your turn in a debate, when someone challenges you, when debates you follow are updated, and when someone comments on your arguments. You can customize notification preferences in your account settings.

Account & Subscription

Go to your Profile, tap "Subscription", and select the plan you want (Mid or Unlimited). You'll be charged through your Apple App Store or Google Play Store account. Subscriptions are billed monthly and can be cancelled anytime.

Yes, you can cancel anytime through your App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) subscription settings. You'll continue to have access to your plan's features until the end of your current billing period. When cancelled, you'll automatically downgrade to the Free plan.

Go to your Profile, tap "Settings", then "Change Password". You'll need to verify your identity by entering your current password before setting a new one. Passwords must be at least 8 characters and include a number and special character.

Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your debates, comments, and data will be deleted. If you have an active subscription, make sure to cancel it first through your app store. For more information, visit our Account Deletion page.

Privacy & Safety

We take privacy seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt. We never share your personal information with third parties. For more details, read our Privacy Policy.

Yes. You can report any debate or comment that violates our community guidelines by tapping the report button. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours. We have zero tolerance for hate speech, harassment, or harmful content.

Hash it Out is built on respect and intellectual honesty. We prohibit: hate speech, personal attacks, harassment, spam, misinformation presented as fact, and any illegal content. Debates should focus on ideas, not individuals. Violations may result in account suspension or removal.

Technical
