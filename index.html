How It Works

From challenge to conclusion in four simple steps

1

Create or Join a Debate

Start a debate on any topic, challenge a specific user, or browse open debates and apply to participate. Choose your side and set the stage for intelligent discourse.

2

Present Your Argument

Craft your position with supporting evidence. Add sources, cite your claims, and build a compelling case. You have 7 days to research and respond.

3

Engage in Turn-Based Exchange

Respond to your opponent's arguments, address their evidence, and refine your position. The community can follow along, vote, and comment.

4

Conclude with Insights

End the debate when consensus is reached or arguments are exhausted. Review AI-generated summaries and community votes to see which arguments resonated.
