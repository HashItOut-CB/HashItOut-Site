Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Learn how to delete your account and data.

At Hash it Out, you have complete control over your account and data. This page explains how to request account deletion and what happens to your data when your account is deleted.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete In-App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly through the Hash it Out mobile app:

  1. Open the Hash it Out app
  2. Navigate to your Profile
  3. Tap on Settings
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision by entering your password
  6. Your account will be permanently deleted

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by email:

  1. Send an email to support@hashitout.net
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the following information:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your username (if you remember it)
    • A brief confirmation that you want to delete your account
  4. We will verify your identity and process your request within 72 hours
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted

Email Template:

To: support@hashitout.net
Subject: Account Deletion Request

Hello,

I would like to request the deletion of my Hash it Out account.

Email: [your registered email]
Username: [your username]

Please confirm when my account has been deleted.

Thank you.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following information is permanently removed from our systems:

Personal Information

  • Your username, email address, and password
  • Your profile information (name, bio, profile picture)
  • Your authentication tokens and session data
  • Your notification preferences and settings
  • Your push notification tokens

User-Generated Content

  • All debates you created or participated in
  • All your arguments, evidence, and sources
  • All your comments and replies
  • Your votes and ratings on debates
  • Your follower/following relationships
  • Your saved or bookmarked debates

Subscription Information

  • Your subscription history and payment records
  • Your subscription tier and expiration date
  • Note: Active subscriptions will be cancelled, but you should also cancel through the App Store or Google Play to stop future billing

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I reactivate my account after deletion?

No. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You would need to create a new account, and all your previous data will be lost.

Will other users see my debates after I delete my account?

No. All debates, arguments, and comments you created will be permanently removed from the platform.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To stop future billing:

  • iOS: Open Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions > Hash it Out > Cancel Subscription
  • Android: Open Google Play > Menu > Subscriptions > Hash it Out > Cancel Subscription

What if I delete my account by mistake?

Contact us immediately at support@hashitout.net. If you contact us within 24 hours of deletion, we may be able to restore your account from backups. After 24 hours, recovery is not possible.

How long does the deletion process take?

If you delete via the app, it's immediate. If you request deletion via email, we'll process your request within 72 hours.

Will I receive confirmation when my account is deleted?

Yes. You will receive a confirmation email at your registered email address once your account has been deleted.

Data Download Before Deletion

If you'd like a copy of your data before deleting your account, you can request a data export:

  1. Email privacy@hashitout.net with the subject "Data Export Request"
  2. We will provide you with a downloadable copy of your data within 30 days
  3. The export will include all your debates, comments, profile information, and activity history
