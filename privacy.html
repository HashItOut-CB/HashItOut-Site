Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 22, 2025

At Hash it Out, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (stored as an encrypted hash using bcrypt)
  • First and last name (optional)

1.2 Content You Create

We store content you voluntarily provide:

  • Debate topics, arguments, and evidence
  • Comments and replies
  • Votes and ratings
  • Profile information

1.3 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our app:

  • Device type, operating system, and version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address
  • App usage statistics and feature interactions
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Push notification tokens (for sending you notifications)

1.4 Information from Third Parties

We receive limited information from:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: Transaction details for subscription purchases
  • Firebase: Analytics and crash reporting data
  • OpenAI: We send debate content to OpenAI's API for AI analysis (summaries and bias detection). This content is processed according to OpenAI's data policies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the service: Create and manage your account, authenticate users, and deliver app functionality
  • Process subscriptions: Manage your subscription tier and billing through Apple/Google
  • Enable debates: Facilitate turn-based debates, match users, and display content
  • AI features: Generate debate summaries and analyze evidence bias using OpenAI's GPT-4
  • Send notifications: Alert you about your turn in debates, challenges, comments, and important updates
  • Improve the app: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • Ensure safety: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our terms
  • Communicate with you: Send account-related emails (verification, password resets) and respond to support requests

3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

3.1 Public Content

Content you post in debates and comments is publicly visible to other app users. This includes:

  • Your username and profile
  • Debate arguments and evidence
  • Comments and votes
  • Follower/following relationships

3.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the app:

  • Google Cloud Platform: Hosts our servers and database
  • Firebase: Push notifications, analytics, and crash reporting
  • OpenAI: Processes debate content for AI summaries and bias analysis
  • Apple & Google: Process subscription payments

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal requests (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Enforcement of our Terms of Service
  • Protection of our rights, safety, or property
  • Investigation of fraud or security issues

3.4 Business Transfers

If Hash it Out is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you of any such change in ownership or control of your personal information.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Password security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with automatic salting
  • Authentication: JWT tokens with 1-hour expiry for secure session management
  • SQL injection prevention: Parameterized queries for all database operations
  • Rate limiting: Protection against brute force attacks
  • Regular security audits: Continuous monitoring and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

5.1 Access and Portability

You can access and export your data by contacting us at privacy@hashitout.net. We will provide you with a copy of your personal information in a structured, commonly used format.

5.2 Correction

You can update your profile information directly in the app. For other corrections, contact us at support@hashitout.net.

5.3 Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the app (Profile > Settings > Delete Account) or by following the instructions on our Account Deletion page. This will permanently remove:

  • Your account and profile information
  • Your debates and arguments
  • Your comments and votes
  • Your following/follower relationships

Note: Some information may be retained in our backup systems for up to 90 days. For detailed information about the deletion process, visit hashitout.net/delete-account.

5.4 Opt-Out of Communications

You can manage notification preferences in the app settings. You cannot opt out of essential account-related emails (e.g., verification, security alerts) while maintaining an account.

5.5 Do Not Track

Our app does not respond to Do Not Track signals as there is no industry standard for compliance.

6. Children's Privacy

Hash it Out is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@hashitout.net.

7. International Data Transfers

Hash it Out is operated from the United Kingdom. If you access our services from outside the UK, your information will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the UK and other countries where our service providers operate.

By using Hash it Out, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection laws.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Specifically:

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Debate content: Retained as long as the debate exists on the platform
  • Usage logs: Retained for 90 days for security and debugging purposes
  • Deleted account data: Removed from production systems within 30 days, but may persist in backups for up to 90 days

We may retain certain information longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention, legal disputes).

9. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any information.

10. Analytics and Cookies

10.1 Mobile Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our app. This helps us improve features and fix issues. Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated.

10.2 Cookies

Our mobile app does not use cookies. However, if you access our website (hashitout.net), we may use essential cookies for basic functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

11. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose
  • Right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the "sale" of your personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@hashitout.net with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

12. GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or UK, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@hashitout.net. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending you a notification through the app or email

Your continued use of Hash it Out after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@hashitout.net

General Support: support@hashitout.net

Website: https://hashitout.net

We are committed to resolving privacy concerns in a timely manner and typically respond within 72 hours.
